Mobile Patrol

Our team will arrive onsite and conduct a thorough assessment of your premises, checking for any signs of vulnerability that could allow issues of vandalism, theft, damage, or squatting. Our mobilisation team are experts in their field and will ensure a bespoke approach for your needs.

We are renowned for the quality and expertise of our mobile patrol security services ensuring every security officer is highly trained and fully licensed to handle whatever situations the job demands. We offer additional training in first aid, fire and client centric adaptations.

Manned Guard

Acting as a highly visible deterrent, manned security guarding is still one of the most effective measures to ensure the protection of your premises and personnel. All our personnel are trained to the highest standard and often double as concierge services.

Manned security guards can be located within a client's control room that will also house a CCTV system, and their duties include regular security checks around the site, all of which are recorded. Manned security guards can be employed on a flexible basis, so your business is protected when it is most at risk.

If you need any more information about this service, please, don't hesitate to Contact Us.
