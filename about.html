ISO9001

S&E Security Ltd holds the ISO9001 certificate.

ISO 9001 is the International Standard for Quality Management Systems, first published in 1987 by the International Organisation for Standardisation (ISO), it is one of the most commonly used management tools across the world.

It is designed to help organisations ensure that they meet the needs of customers and other stakeholders, by having a framework in place that helps ensure consistent quality in the provision of goods and/or services.

This standard uses a process approach and is based on 7 quality management principles, which include having a strong customer focus, the involvement of top management, and a drive for continual improvement.

Health and Safety

We at S&E Security Ltd recognise our accountability for the health and safety of our security officers, staff in general, our clients and other people who may be affected by our activities. Our policy is to deliver and maintain a safe and healthy working environment. This includes the equipment we use and the systems of work for all our employees.

We ensure that correct training, management and information is available as and when required, our activities are managed in accordance with current UK health and safety legislation. This ensures that, to the best of our ability, our employees and other people in contact with us, are not exposed to unnecessary risks at any given situation.

Mission Statement

The mission of S&E Security Ltd is to prevent crime against commercial enterprise, to help our clients keep their businesses secure, to protect multi numbers of companies on commercial Trading & Industrial Estates against the ravages of crime and vandalism.

The effect of our work is to provide peace of mind to Company Directors and Managers, cut out the losses caused by crime and vandalism and the financial and psychological aftermath.

The secondary effect of our mission is that we are able to provide on site security, on or close to the industrial areas we protect, with immediate attention to labour problems that larger companies are unable to match.

This whole ethos of our work is environmentally beneficial to all companies within the estates we protect.

Environmental Policy

At S&E Security Ltd We like to think that we are an environmentally friendly company.

As a manned guarding company we do not manufacture anything nor do we use anything that is environmentally undesirable, other than vehicle fuel. We have looked into the possibility of using electric/gas fuelled vehicles and have concluded that this is not a viable option at this stage.

However, our work in industrial estate protection is an advantage to the environment in that valuable resources are not wasted or used up in repairing and replacing damaged building or stolen equipment.

A lot of the estates we protect show an increased pride in their surroundings as the occupants see that vandalism is virtually non-existent and it is worth caring for their buildings, fences and surroundings.

Wherever possible we will recycle all paper we are mindful of electricity wastage and ensure that power supplies and lighting are not used unnecessarily or left on when or premises are empty.
